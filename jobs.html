Green Jobs & Careers

Positions that support environmental sustainability and smart city development.

Sustainability Manager

Lead and coordinate sustainability initiatives across the city, set KPI targets for carbon reduction, run corporate programs, and report on ESG performance.

Environmental Manager

Oversee regulatory compliance, environmental impact assessments, and mitigation plans; work closely with local authorities and developers.

Urban Planner

Design sustainable neighborhoods, integrate green corridors, plan for mixed-use development, and ensure resilient infrastructure for climate adaptation.

Sustainability Consultant

Advise businesses and projects on sustainable practices, energy efficiency, and circular economy strategies to meet ESG goals.

Environmental Technician

Collect environmental samples, perform field monitoring of air/water/soil, maintain data records and support remediation activities.
